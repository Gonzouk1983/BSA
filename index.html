The Iconic Motorcycle & Sidecar

As seen in Dad's Army & On The Buses

Historical Info

This is a BSA m21 600cc 1949 (JVB54) motorcycle with sidecar.

TV Appearances

Dad's Army

This motorcycle appeared in multiple episodes as listed below

  • The Captain's Car, Series 5 - Episode 11 and was First broadcast: 8th December 1972
  • Gorilla Warfare, Series 5 - Episode 10 and was First broadcast: 1st December 1972
  • Everybody's Trucking, Series 9 - Episode 1 and was First Broadcast 2nd November 1977

    • On The Buses

    Featured in the classic 1971 film On the Buses, there's a memorable scene featuring a motorcycle with a sidecar. In this scene, Arthur (Michael Robbins) drives the motorcycle, with his wife Olive (Anna Karen) seated in the sidecar. The motorcycle used is a 1949 BSA M21. This particular scene is well-known for its comedic elements, especially when Olive experiences a mishap involving the sidecar.
